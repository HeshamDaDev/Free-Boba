Chapter 1: Where does Boba originate from?

Bubble tea originates from Taiwan somehwere in the 1980s, so fairly recently. However, the individual inventor is unknown. There are however 2 people whom are considered the ones who invented Bubble Tea (Boba), Liu Han-chieh, and Tu Tsung-ho.


Chapter 2: What is it made of?

Tea is a cold drink made with black tea, some type of sweetener like sugar, ice and the famous bubbles, which are tapioca pearls. The aforementioned tapioca pearls, also known as Boba, are made with tapioca, a starch from the cassava plant.
